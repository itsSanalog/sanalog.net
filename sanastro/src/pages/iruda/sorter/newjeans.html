Description

Sorter for NewJeans songs. Pick your albums, and hit the Start button.

Click on the song you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know them.

If you need a break, you can save your progress for another time.



Controls

Before sorting

S: Start sorting
L: Load progress

During sorting

H: Pick left
L: Pick right
K: Tie
J: Undo
S: Save progress
