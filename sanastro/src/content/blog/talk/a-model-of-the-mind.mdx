Will (valuable)
- Love.
- External factors.

Depression (nugatory)
- made of many things.

The conflict between the valuable and the nugatory causes optimisation, alomst like an immune response. Optimisation is bad. We achieve indifference when N=V. This is where peak efficiency happens. When N>V, however, you die. One must choose how much of a buffer to leave, and my buffer is getting uncomfortably small.

If one is aware of the buffer, a change in buffer state induces anxiety, further increasing N and making the change exponential. This is a dangerous positive feedback loop. In fact, this phenomenon can still occur without conscious awareness of our buffer state, resulting in what we observe as spiralling.
